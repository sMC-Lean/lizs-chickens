breeds

Silkie
a smaller chicken, and produces a smaller egg. not known for their laying, these cute chickens are better suited to pets or as brood stock, although they will lay pretty blue eggs.
Brahma
A large Breed, docile and comfortable as a backyard pet. These birds are considered reliable layers and known to produce large Eggs due to their size.
Sussex
Sussex is a very old breed, considered to be reliable layers. they also make great backyard pets as they are generally friendly and easy to handle.
