How It Works

Check availability below or contact me to ask questions.

We periodically update the site with the chickens that are currently available, but you can always use the contact form above to ask us questions, see if we have what you are looking for or for any other general enquiry.

Choose the type and quantity of chickens.

We can work through your selection to ensure you have adequate space and resources, that you are choosing the right chickens for your area and needs, and that you understand what will be required.

Stand by to receive your feathery guests!

Once the chickens are ready and your space is prepared, we'll get your new feathered friends settled in and accustomed to their new home. We will do our best to help you make this transition as smooth as possible.
